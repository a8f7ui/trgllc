About Technology Research Group

Technology Research Group is a professional organization dedicated to advancing practical technology through rigorous investigation, field testing, and applied research. The company works with organizations and stakeholders to evaluate emerging solutions, validate performance in real-world conditions, and identify improvements that enhance reliability, efficiency, and outcomes. From early-stage experimentation to structured testing and technical studies, Technology Research Group helps clients make informed decisions backed by clear, evidence-based findings and actionable recommendations.


Based in Milwaukee, Wisconsin, our work emphasizes objective evaluation, thoughtful analysis, and practical insight. We believe that careful research and measurable results provide a stronger foundation for technology decisions than assumptions or speculation.

Core Areas of Focus

Technology Evaluation

Independent assessment of developing technologies to better understand practical capabilities, limitations, and readiness for operational environments.

Field Testing

Structured observation and validation in real-world conditions to produce meaningful performance data and objective findings.

Applied Research

Research activities focused on practical implementation, measurable outcomes, and informed decision support.

Technical Reporting

Clear documentation, evidence-based conclusions, and actionable recommendations presented in a professional format.

Leadership

Technology Research Group is guided by a leadership team with extensive experience supporting both public sector and private enterprise clients. Our directors have led research, evaluation, and technical advisory engagements across government, institutional, and commercial environments, and understand the standards of rigor, documentation, and discretion those settings require.


This background informs how we operate: engagements are handled with professionalism and confidentiality, findings are documented to withstand scrutiny, and client information is never disclosed without authorization. In keeping with the confidential nature of our work, we do not publish client names or engagement details.
